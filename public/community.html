Community Guidelines

At Prism, we are building a space for thoughtful, respectful, and enriching conversations. These guidelines help keep our community safe.

Be Respectful

Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, hate speech, and personal attacks are strictly prohibited. We are here to learn from each other.

Share Knowledge, Not Noise

Prism is for high-quality "Spreads". Ensure your content adds value, whether it's an article, a tutorial, or an insightful thought.

Authenticity Matters

Do not impersonate others or spread misinformation. Be transparent about your sources and affiliations.

See something wrong?

If you encounter content that violates these rules, please report it immediately.
