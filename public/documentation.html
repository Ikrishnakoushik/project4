Welcome to Prism

Prism is a modern digital publishing platform designed for clarity, depth, and discovery. Whether you're here to read insightful spreads or publish your own, this guide will help you get started.

core Philosophy

We believe in high-quality content over clickbait. A "Spread" is our term for a post or articleâ€”content designed to be spread and shared.

Account & Profile

Your profile is your digital identity on Prism. You can customize your avatar, bio, and display name.

  • Avatar: Upload a square image (JPG/PNG).
  • Bio: Tell the world who you are in 160 characters.
  • Settings: Manage your password and preferences from the Settings tab.

Publisher Studio

Ready to share your voice? The Publisher Studio is a powerful WYSIWYG editor that lets you craft beautiful stories.

To access it, simply click on "Start a new spread..." in the main feed sidebar.

Rich Text Features

Highlight any text to see formatting options: Bold, Italic, Headings, and Quotes.

Images & Videos

Visuals make your story come alive.

Images: You can drag & drop images directly into the editor or use the image button in the toolbar. Once inserted, click an image to align it (Left, Center, Right).

Videos: We support YouTube embeds. Click the video icon and paste the full YouTube URL.
